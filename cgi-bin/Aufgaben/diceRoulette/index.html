Dice Poker - Game Rules

Dice Poker is a local multi-player game where players take turns rolling a die. The goal is to reach 100 points before the opponent.

How to Play:

  • Players take turns rolling the die.
  • A player can roll multiple times in a turn. The sum of all rolls in the turn is added to their total score when they decide to "hold".
  • If a player rolls a "1", they lose all points from that turn and their turn ends immediately.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins.
  • The winner is visually highlighted.
  • A "New Game" button resets the game.
  • The active player is visually indicated.
  • The second player can be replaced by a bot.

Bot Behavior:

  • The bot rolls the die randomly.
  • The bot’s decisions (e.g., "Rolling again" or "Holding") are displayed as text before execution.
