In the tapestry of time, since the seventh of November, A love story unfolds, a tale to remember. Maria, my goddess, my celestial delight, In your presence, every moment feels so right.

Your eyes, a deep brown, like ancient earth's embrace, Hold galaxies of warmth, a captivating grace. In curls of midnight, your hair cascades, An ethereal river, where love serenades.

Soft and kissable, your lips enchant the night, A symphony of passion, a celestial light. Your charm, a potion, brewed with care, A bewitching elixir, beyond compare.

Your personality, a garden of empathy, Blooms with understanding, a rare epiphany. Through the months, like petals unfurl, Our love story, a dance, a romantic swirl.

Each day, a canvas, painted with glee, You're the artist, my love, setting my heart free. Happiness blooms in your radiant smile, Energizing my soul, a love that spans a mile.

Like a day without sun, or chocolate without sweet, Life without you is incomplete. You're my world, my heart's decree, Maria, my love, you mean everything to me.

In this cosmic journey, you're my guiding star, A deity on earth, my love from afar. For all you are and all you do, My gratitude, Maria, I shower on you.

So on this day of love, Valentine's embrace, I declare my love, a timeless grace. Thank you for everything, my sweet dove, Happy Valentine's Day, my goddess, my love.



In the quiet morn, your presence beams, A radiant light that fills my dreams. Pride and glee, your love imparts, No need for light with you in my heart.

A smile that outshines the morning sun, A thousand stars in your eyes, each one. Short black curls frame a face so fair, Divine beauty, a masterpiece rare.

In every aspect, your kindness defined, Apart, I sense the ache and yearn. For your love, in every twist and turn, Morning whispers of love we share.

A symphony of hearts, an eternal pair, Maria, my love, in each day's start, Forever entwined, never apart.

Maria, my babe, love of my life, In my heart, you shine so bright. I'm sorry for the pain I caused, Each hurtful word, a love temporarily paused.

I see the tears in your eyes, Regret echoes in my silent sighs. I wasn't there when you needed me most, A lapse in love, a regretful ghost.

I hurt you again, my deepest remorse, I promise, love, to change my course. In the silence, hear my plea, I love you with all of me.

I'm sorry for the way you feel, In my heart, this love is real. Forgive my faults, my love so true, Maria, I'm sorry, I love you.



In the dance of time, my love, you shine, A woman almost divine, forever mine. Each second without you, a quake within, Dependent on your love, where I begin.

Hurting in bones, longing for your embrace, Earthly tasks bind In your eyes, I see a love so pure and deep, A treasure hidden, a secret that you keep. Your heart, a wellspring of joy that never ends, In your love, Maria, my heart transcends.

Perfect in every way, that's what you are, A shining, brilliant, unattainable star. Your love fills my life with endless delight, In your presence, everything feels just right.

Maria, you're the embodiment of my dreams, In your love, reality and fantasy gleams. Perfection personified in every single part, You've captured my soul, you've won my heart.

In your embrace, I've found my home and more, With you, my love, I'll eternally explore. ough life's ups and downs, hand in hand, we'll trod, For you, Maria, are truly a gift from the gods.



In this messy life, I yearn to break free, To promise a change, a vow to be. Consumption's hold, a struggle within, A battle I fight, hoping love will win.

No guarantees, just a heartfelt plea, To be with you for all eternity. In this imperfect dance, our love will last, A promise unspoken, but held steadfast.
