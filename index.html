Hi, I'm Luca Burger, a 17-year-old Swiss student with a passion for technology and the great outdoors. Born on November 16, 2005, I'm currently attending the Informatik Mittelschule (IMS) in Sargans.

Interstellar Development

A huge thanks to my friend Patrick, who kept pushing me to code and supporting me throughout this journey. Without his encouragement, this project wouldn't be where it is today.

Interstellar Development is driven by our passion for free and open-source software. The Free Software Foundation (FSF) has been a major inspiration for us. Their philosophy on user freedom and open collaboration deeply influences our work, and we strive to carry that vision forward with every project we undertake.

What we're trying to achieve is simple but important: to build high-quality software that is freely available to everyone, and to create a space where open-source gaming can thrive. We want to develop games and tools that are not only accessible across platforms but also built on principles of openness and community.
